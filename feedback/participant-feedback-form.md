# Participant Feedback Form — Isambard 3 Practical Workshop

## Intro text (shown at the top of the form)

Thank you for attending the Isambard 3 Practical Workshop on 21 April 2026.

This form takes about **4–5 minutes**. There are no right answers and no trick questions. We are not measuring our own
performance — we are trying to understand what would actually be useful to you and people like you, so that future
sessions are better matched to real needs.

One thing we already know: the workshop tried to serve too wide a range of experience levels at once. We are planning to
offer **separate sessions pitched at different levels** in future runs. With that in mind, your answers can focus on
what would genuinely help you, rather than on that particular limitation.

If you have questions or need support with Isambard 3, email us at **isambard-support@exeter.ac.uk** — this is open to
all University of Exeter users, including prospective ones.

Responses are anonymous.
